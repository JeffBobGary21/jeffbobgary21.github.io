At Brooms Head

13th Of June

We decided to travel to Brooms Head for a family outing.

The Brooms Head hike is a path that goes from the lookout to a beach. The start of the path is located here:

The path can be seen on the left side of the map.

The path goes all the way down to a beach where you could swim or you could climb the massive sand wall to get some great photos, like I did!

We went on a hike, the same one that they do at school for the year 7-8 camp.
